Question 1: Match the term

Read the definition of each term below, then match it to the correct statement in your book.

Even Numbers: These are numbers that can be divided by 2 exactly, with no remainder (e.g., 2, 4, 6, ...).
Odd Numbers: These are numbers that have a remainder of 1 when divided by 2 (e.g., 1, 3, 5, ...).
Fibonacci Numbers: This is a special sequence where you get the next term by adding the two terms before it. It starts with 0, 1, 1, 2, 3, 5, ...
Pascal's Triangle: This is a triangular arrangement of numbers where each number is the sum of the two numbers directly above it. The outside edges are all 1s.
