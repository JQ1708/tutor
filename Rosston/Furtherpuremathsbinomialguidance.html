Chapter 10: Binomial Series Guidance

Binomial Expansion Formula:
For (1 + x)ⁿ, where n is not a positive integer:

(1 + x)ⁿ = 1 + nx + [n(n-1)/2!]x² + [n(n-1)(n-2)/3!]x³ + ...

The expansion is valid for |x| < 1.
