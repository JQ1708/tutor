  • Solution: The blue colour remains constant because Cu2+ ions removed at the cathode are replaced by the anode dissolving.

Quick Revision: Electrolysis Rules (Aqueous)

  • Cathode (-): The less reactive element (metal or hydrogen) is discharged. If the metal is more reactive than hydrogen (e.g., Na, Mg), Hydrogen is produced. If less reactive (e.g., Cu, Ag), the Metal is produced.
