Balanced & Unbalanced Forces

  • Force: A push, pull, or twist. Measured in Newtons (N).
  • Balanced Forces: Equal in size, opposite in direction. Cause no change in movement.
  • Unbalanced Forces: Unequal in size or not opposite. Always cause a change in movement.

Force Diagrams

Force Diagrams

  • Arrows: Represent forces.
  • Arrow Direction: Shows the force's direction.
  • Arrow Length: Represents the force's size. A longer arrow means a bigger force.
